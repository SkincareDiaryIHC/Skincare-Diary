BEAUTY


CONSULTANT

The app will help you find the best, most trustworthy beauty consultants, who will recommend you quality products and ensure a better care of your skin.

THROUGH THE APP YOU WILL BE ABLE TO:

FILTER BY BRAND

For a more efficient search, you will be able to filter the beauty consultants by the brand they work for.

FILTER BY SCHEDULE

For a more efficient search, you will be able to filter the beauty consultants by their available schedules.

AS A CONSULTANT YOU WILL BE ABLE TO ACCESS:

APPOINTMENTS

You will be able to see the date and time of the appointments that each client takes, as well as see their respective profiles to better advise them.

SCHEDULE

You will be able to edit and save your schedule, according to the spots you have available, and will in turn be accessible for clients to choose an appointment with you.
